\# Annex E — Certification Revocation Semantics



Revocation is:

• Automatic

• Retroactive

• Public

• Non-appealable



Triggers:

• Silence violation

• Termination breach

• Fraud

• Unverifiable silence

• Systematic failure



Entity Effects:

• Immediate suspension

• Cascade audits

• Permanent disqualification if repeated



Meta-Principle:

Certification authority is temporary and falsifiable.
